Our Technology

CBCT Imaging & Diagnostics

Our low-radiation CBCT imaging provides precise 3D views of root canal anatomy for accurate diagnosis and planning—minimizing guesswork and improving outcomes.

  • High-resolution 3D visualization
  • Targeted field of view to reduce exposure
  • Improved detection of complex anatomy

Operating Microscope

Microscopic magnification and illumination allow us to treat fine, complex canals with maximum precision while preserving healthy tooth structure.

  • Enhanced visibility for intricate anatomy
  • More conservative, precise treatment
  • Improved long-term success

Rotary Endodontic Systems

Advanced rotary instrumentation streamlines treatment, improves cleaning efficiency, and creates consistent canal shapes for reliable sealing.

  • Efficient canal shaping
  • Smoother, faster procedures
  • Consistent results

Sterilization & Safety Protocols

Comprehensive infection control with Class B autoclaves, ultrasonic cleaning, and continuous monitoring ensures a safe clinical environment.

  • Medical-grade sterilization standards
  • Traceable validation and monitoring
  • Dedicated instrument workflows
