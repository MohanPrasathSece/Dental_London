Our Services

Specialised Root Canal Therapy & Comprehensive Dental Hygiene

Root Canal Therapy Excellence

Root canal therapy is our specialty and passion. When the inner part of your tooth becomes infected or severely damaged, a root canal can save your natural tooth and eliminate pain. Our advanced techniques ensure comfortable, efficient treatment with excellent long-term results.

We use the latest rotary endodontic systems, digital imaging, and microscopic precision to provide the highest standard of care. Most treatments can be completed in one or two comfortable visits.

Don't let tooth pain control your life. Our expert root canal therapy can restore your oral health and preserve your natural smile.

Our Specialised Services

Primary Root Canal Therapy

Save your natural tooth when the pulp is infected or inflamed. We remove infected tissue, disinfect with microscopic precision, and seal the canals to prevent reinfection. Most cases finish in one or two comfortable visits.

Root Canal Retreatment

When a previous treatment hasn’t fully healed, we re‑access the tooth, locate missed anatomy, and re‑clean and seal using updated techniques and materials to improve long‑term success.

Emergency Root Canal Care

Rapid relief for severe toothache, swelling, or dental trauma. We prioritise same‑day appointments to stabilise the tooth, control pain, and begin definitive care.

Specialist Consultation

A thorough review including history, examination, and digital imaging. We explain findings clearly and provide a personalised treatment plan with transparent timelines and fees.

Microscopic Endodontics

All endodontic procedures are performed under high magnification for accuracy—helping to find hidden canals, preserve healthy tooth structure, and enhance outcomes.

CBCT Imaging & Diagnosis

3D imaging (where indicated) offers precise views of root anatomy, lesions, and previous treatments, improving diagnosis and guiding minimally invasive care.

Comprehensive Dental Hygiene

Maintaining excellent oral hygiene is crucial for preventing dental problems and supporting overall health. Our dental hygiene services go beyond basic cleaning to provide comprehensive care tailored to your individual needs.

Our experienced hygienists use the latest techniques and equipment to ensure thorough cleaning while maintaining your comfort. We also provide personalised education to help you maintain optimal oral health at home.

Regular hygiene appointments are an investment in your long-term oral health and can help prevent the need for more extensive treatments in the future.

What to Expect

1

Initial Consultation

Your first visit includes a comprehensive examination, digital X‑rays if needed, and a detailed discussion of your symptoms and treatment options. We take time to answer all your questions and ensure you are comfortable with the proposed treatment plan.

2

Treatment Process

Most root canal treatments are completed in one to two visits. We use advanced anaesthesia techniques to ensure your comfort throughout the procedure. Our modern equipment allows for precise, efficient treatment with minimal discomfort.

3

Post‑treatment Care

We provide detailed aftercare instructions and are available for any questions or concerns following your treatment. Follow‑up appointments ensure proper healing and long‑term success.

4

Pain Management

We understand that dental anxiety is common, especially for root canal procedures. We offer various comfort measures and pain management options to ensure your experience is as comfortable as possible.

5

Advanced Technology

Our state‑of‑the‑art equipment includes digital imaging, rotary endodontic systems, and microscopic visualisation tools that enhance precision and reduce treatment time.

6

Long-term Success

Our goal is not just to treat your immediate problem, but to ensure long-term success. We provide guidance on maintaining your oral health and preventing future issues.
