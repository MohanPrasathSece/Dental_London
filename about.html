About Our Practice

Dedicated to Excellence in Root Canal Therapy

Our Ethos

Precision Root Canal Therapy was established to deliver the highest standard of endodontic care, while prioritising patient comfort and satisfaction.

Our practice blends years of specialist training with the latest technology and materials with a friendly and ethical ethos.

We also take pride in providing local post-graduate dental education and supporting referring practitioners in enhancing the quality of care they offer.

Our Expertise

Chetan Morjaria

Specialist in Endodontics

BDS (Lon) Dip.MJDF Dip Phil MClinDent (Endo, with distinction) MEndo RCS (Ed)

GDC Number: 193203

Chetan is one of only a few hundred dentists in the UK who are registered as Specialists in Endodontics by the UK’s General Dental Council.

Chetan qualified as a dentist from King’s College London in 2010. After his degree he completed his Vocational Training in Surrey where he developed his interest in dentistry.

He went on to undertake numerous hospital posts as a Senior House Officer, where he gained further training under consultant‑led tutelage. This included Restorative Dentistry, Special Care dentistry, Oral and Maxillofacial Surgery, and Orthodontics at Kingston, East Surrey and Croydon University Hospitals. He has also had considerable experience providing emergency dentistry to patients in South East London.

Chetan went on to complete his postgraduate specialist training at the prestigious King’s College London Dental Institute. He was involved in teaching undergraduate dental students at Guy’s Hospital and lectures to general dentists on root canal treatment.

He regularly attends postgraduate courses to keep abreast of the latest developments, and finds satisfaction in using his experience in various specialities of dentistry to provide comprehensive and holistic dental care. We are pleased to have him on board, and many patients have commented on his caring and gentle manner.

Outside of dentistry, Chetan enjoys fitness, spending time with family and friends and regularly carries out voluntary work in London and abroad.
