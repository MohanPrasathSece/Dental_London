Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accept insurance?

We work with most major dental insurance providers. Please contact us with your insurance details, and we'll verify your coverage before your appointment.

How do I prepare for my appointment?

Please bring a valid ID, insurance information, and a list of any medications you're taking. If you're experiencing pain, you may take over-the-counter pain relievers as directed.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we offer flexible payment options to make your treatment affordable. We'll discuss payment plans during your consultation.

What should I expect during my first visit?

Your first visit includes a comprehensive examination, digital X-rays if needed, and a detailed discussion of your treatment options. We'll answer all your questions and create a personalized treatment plan.

How long does a root canal procedure take?

Most root canal treatments can be completed in one to two appointments, each lasting 60-90 minutes. Complex cases may require additional visits.

Is root canal treatment painful?

With modern techniques and anesthesia, root canal treatment is typically no more uncomfortable than having a filling. We prioritize your comfort throughout the procedure.
